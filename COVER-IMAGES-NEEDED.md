## ðŸŽ¨ Image Guidelines

**Style:**
- Sexy, exciting, professionally designed
- Should evoke the genre/trope combination
- Dark/moody aesthetic for Dark Romance and Romantasy
- Bright/contemporary feel for Contemporary
- Clear, readable even at small sizes

**Technical:**
- Format: PNG or JPG (both work)
- Recommended size: 800x1200px or similar (portrait/vertical)
- File size: Keep under 500KB for fast loading

**Naming Convention:**
```
{genre-slug}_{first-trope-slug}.png
```

Where:
- `genre-slug` = lowercase genre with hyphens (e.g., "dark-romance")
- `first-trope-slug` = lowercase first trope with hyphens (e.g., "enemies-to-lovers")
